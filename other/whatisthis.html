What is this?

It's a webcomic.

More specifically, it's a webcomic in the style of those previously hosted at mspaintadventures.com (now defunct and redirecting to homestuck.com). A site hosted by american comic writter Andrew Hussie, creator of the infamous webcomic internet phenomena Homestuck.

Readers enter commands for the characters to perform (over the sofa discord server in this case) and I draw some silly panels and write what happens then, sometimes I might take over the narrative to advance the plot forwards, but that's about it.

Sometimes pages might have sound, which will be indicated by an "[S]" before the command.
